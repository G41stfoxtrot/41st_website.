About Us

Geetsly's 41st Elite Corps is a Star Wars Military Simulation (MilSim) community centered around Battlefront II. We pride ourselves on camaraderie, leadership, and individuality. Join us to relive the Clone Wars on the frontlines as a Clone Trooper, evolving through trials and challenges. Our community also hosts movie nights for the newest Star Wars shows and engages in various games like Minecraft, Enlisted, and Battlefield. Enlistment is required for those who love Battlefront and seek a dedicated community.

FAQ

What platforms do you support?

We have company on PC, PlayStation, and Xbox, ensuring inclusivity across gaming platforms.

Is enlistment mandatory?

Enlistment is required for those who love Battlefront and want a dedicated community to join. However, we also offer casual activities for non-enlisted members.

Ranking System

Our ranking system is designed to recognize dedication and skill within the community. Members can progress through various ranks by participating in events, demonstrating leadership, and contributing to the community's growth. Detailed information about each rank and the criteria for promotion is available to members upon joining.

Python Regiment

The Python Regiment is a specialized unit within the 41st Elite Corps, focusing on advanced tactics and strategic operations. Members of this regiment undergo rigorous training and participate in high-stakes missions, embodying the elite nature of our community.
